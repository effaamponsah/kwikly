All components must have camelCase

- This is a simple food ordering app that allows people to order food from
nearby restaurants and pay for them via momo or by delivery

- Users get the chance to reorder previous order
- Leave a comment for the food or rate it.
- Users can also order the food and pick it up later
- Only registered users are allowed to use the system.
- Add google login to it
- Receipts are sent to the email provided
